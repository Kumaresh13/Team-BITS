About Us

Hailing from Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani campus we are a team of dedicated engineers and science majors who share a mutual love for automobiles and the environment and what better opportunity than Shell-Eco Marathon to showcase our zeal.

We aim to build the most fuel efficient vehicles with the most sustainable materials and make the planet a beautiful abode to live in. We have been active since 2012 and till now have won accolades both nationally and internationally. Additionally we boast of being the first Indian college team to build an Ethanol based car engine. We are constantly working to improve our game and give back to the community in every way possible which mostly includes being vocal about saving our planet Earth.
